Empowering Courses

Popular Six-Week Courses

First Aid Basics

This First Aid Basic course will introduce you to the fundamentals and practical skills required to be confident in this field. Practical activities and assessments included.

Lessons
  • Lesson 1: Emergency response and CPR basics — how to assess a scene, call for help and perform CPR.
  • Lesson 2: Wound care & bandaging — cleaning wounds, dressing techniques and infection prevention.

Organic Gardening

This Organic Gardening course will introduce you to the fundamentals and practical skills required to be confident in this field. Practical activities and assessments included.

Lessons
  • Lesson 1: Soil health & composting — understand soil structure, compost making, and natural fertilizers.
  • Lesson 2: Planting & pest control — seasonal planting, companion planting and eco-friendly pest management.

Advanced Cooking

This Advanced Cooking course will introduce you to the fundamentals and practical skills required to be confident in this field. Practical activities and assessments included.

Lessons
  • Lesson 1: Knife skills & ingredient prep — learn safe knife handling, chopping, and mise en place techniques used in professional kitchens.
  • Lesson 2: Sauces & flavour building — basic mother sauces, seasoning, and techniques to balance flavours.

Popular Short Courses

Pediatric First Aid

This Pediatric First Aid course will introduce you to the fundamentals and practical skills required to be confident in this field, focusing on child-specific care. Practical activities and assessments included.

Lessons
  • Lesson 1: Child & infant CPR — learn the specific techniques for performing CPR and using an AED on children and infants.
  • Lesson 2: Common childhood emergencies — managing choking, allergic reactions, fevers, and injuries common to young children.

Basic Sewing

This Basic Sewing course will introduce you to the fundamentals and practical skills required to be confident in this field. Practical activities and assessments included.

Lessons
  • Lesson 1: Machine basics & stitching — how to thread a sewing machine, adjust tension, and sew straight, curved, and corner stitches.
  • Lesson 2: Simple repairs & projects — learn to mend seams, sew on buttons, and complete a simple project like a pillowcase or tote bag.

Landscaping Design

This Landscaping course will introduce you to the fundamentals and practical skills required to be confident in this field. Practical activities and assessments included.

Lessons
  • Lesson 1: Garden design basics — space planning, plant selection, and focal points.
  • Lesson 2: Hardscaping & maintenance — basic pathways, edging, and seasonal maintenance tips.

About Us

What We Do?

We provide nutritious meals to elderly and homebound individuals who may not be able to shop for or prepare their own food.

Who Are We?

A registered Non-Profit Organisation (NPO) that delivers balanced meals and provides community support to vulnerable groups.

Our Impact

Preventing isolation, improving health, and reducing hospital admissions for seniors and vulnerable populations.
